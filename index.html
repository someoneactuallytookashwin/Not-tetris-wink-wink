HOW TO PLAY

Arrange falling blocks to fill horizontal lines!

Complete lines disappear and give you points.

Game gets faster as you level up.

Use the preview to plan ahead!

Press ENTER for controls

CONTROLS

SPACE - Rotate

A - Move left

S - Move down

D - Move right

R - Restart
